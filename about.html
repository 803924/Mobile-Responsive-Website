What is Track and field


Track and field is a sport where there's a track, and there's a field just like in the name, what a coincident. there's a buch of diffrent events that you that include running on the track, and jumping and throwing on the field. All these events are generally individual (with relays as an exception) where people are competing against eachother to see who's the best.

The image show above is a layout of the track. The track is the orange part outside the green (which is grass). All running and hurdling events will take place on this track which is 400 meters long. The grass on the inside is where the throwing and jumping events take place. A lot of tracks are around football fields to save space on school campuses, Although some nicer schools will have them seperated.

Scoring gnerally works based off how you place. with the top 8 places scored: 10, 8, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, and 1 to indicate points from first through eighth place. Each male and female division will be scored seperatly.

Most track meetes have the same events. The button below will take you to a page that lists the main track and field events that you will see at meets.
